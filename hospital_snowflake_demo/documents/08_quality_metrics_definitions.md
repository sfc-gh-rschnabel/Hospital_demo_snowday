# Hospital Quality Metrics and KPI Definitions

## Document Information
- **Policy Number**: QM-001
- **Effective Date**: January 1, 2024
- **Review Date**: January 1, 2025
- **Department**: Quality Improvement
- **Approved By**: Dr. Patricia Davis, Chief Quality Officer

## Purpose
To define and standardize quality metrics and Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) used for hospital performance measurement, benchmarking, and continuous improvement initiatives.

## Patient Safety Metrics

### Hospital-Acquired Infections (HAIs)

#### Central Line-Associated Bloodstream Infection (CLABSI)
- **Definition**: BSI in patient with central line at time of infection or within 48 hours of line removal
- **Calculation**: Number of CLABSIs ÷ Central Line Days × 1,000
- **Target**: 0 (Zero Harm goal)
- **National Benchmark**: 0.8 per 1,000 central line days
- **Reporting**: Monthly to Quality Committee, CMS

#### Catheter-Associated Urinary Tract Infection (CAUTI)
- **Definition**: UTI in patient with indwelling urinary catheter > 2 days at time of infection
- **Calculation**: Number of CAUTIs ÷ Catheter Days × 1,000
- **Target**: 0 (Zero Harm goal)
- **National Benchmark**: 1.1 per 1,000 catheter days
- **Reporting**: Monthly to Quality Committee, CMS

#### Surgical Site Infection (SSI)
- **Definition**: Infection at or near surgical incision within 30 days (or 90 days for implants)
- **Calculation**: Number of SSIs ÷ Number of Surgeries × 100
- **Target**: Below national benchmark by procedure type
- **Reporting**: Monthly to Quality Committee, CMS

#### Clostridioides difficile Infection (CDI)
- **Definition**: Healthcare facility-onset C. diff infection
- **Calculation**: Number of HO-CDIs ÷ Patient Days × 10,000
- **Target**: Below national benchmark
- **National Benchmark**: 6.8 per 10,000 patient days
- **Reporting**: Monthly to Quality Committee, CMS

### Patient Falls
- **Definition**: Unplanned descent to floor with or without injury
- **Calculation**: Number of Falls ÷ Patient Days × 1,000
- **Target**: < 3.0 per 1,000 patient days
- **Fall with Injury Rate**: < 0.5 per 1,000 patient days
- **Reporting**: Monthly

### Medication Errors
- **Definition**: Any preventable event that may cause inappropriate medication use
- **Calculation**: Number of Medication Errors ÷ Doses Administered × 100
- **Target**: < 0.5%
- **Serious Medication Errors**: 0
- **Reporting**: Monthly

## Clinical Outcome Metrics

### Mortality Rates

#### Risk-Adjusted Mortality Rate
- **Definition**: Observed mortality ÷ Expected mortality (based on severity adjustment)
- **Target**: < 1.0 (better than expected)
- **Reporting**: Quarterly

#### Condition-Specific Mortality
| Condition | Target | Benchmark |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| AMI (Heart Attack) | < 12% | National avg 11.8% |
| Heart Failure | < 10% | National avg 9.5% |
| Pneumonia | < 10% | National avg 9.1% |
| Stroke | < 12% | National avg 11.3% |

### Readmission Rates

#### 30-Day All-Cause Readmission
- **Definition**: Unplanned readmission within 30 days of discharge
- **Calculation**: 30-day readmissions ÷ Index admissions × 100
- **Target**: < 10%
- **National Benchmark**: 15.5%
- **Reporting**: Monthly

#### Condition-Specific Readmissions
| Condition | Target | Benchmark |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Heart Failure | < 20% | National avg 21.9% |
| AMI | < 15% | National avg 16.1% |
| Pneumonia | < 15% | National avg 17.1% |
| COPD | < 18% | National avg 19.6% |

## Operational Efficiency Metrics

### Length of Stay (LOS)

#### Average Length of Stay (ALOS)
- **Definition**: Average number of days patients remain in hospital
- **Calculation**: Total patient days ÷ Total discharges
- **Target**: Within Case Mix Index (CMI) adjusted benchmark
- **Reporting**: Weekly and monthly

#### Observed vs Expected LOS
- **Definition**: Ratio comparing actual LOS to severity-adjusted expected LOS
- **Calculation**: Observed ALOS ÷ Expected ALOS
- **Target**: < 1.0 (shorter than expected)
- **Reporting**: Monthly

### Emergency Department Metrics

#### NEAT Compliance (4-Hour Target)
- **Definition**: Percentage of ED patients with total ED time ≤ 4 hours
- **Calculation**: ED visits ≤ 4 hours ÷ Total ED visits × 100
- **Target**: ≥ 90%
- **Reporting**: Daily, Weekly, Monthly

#### Door-to-Provider Time
- **Definition**: Time from ED arrival to first provider contact
- **Calculation**: Median time (minutes)
- **Target**: < 30 minutes (ESI 3)
- **Reporting**: Daily

#### Left Without Being Seen (LWBS)
- **Definition**: Patients who leave ED before physician evaluation
- **Calculation**: LWBS patients ÷ Total ED visits × 100
- **Target**: < 2%
- **Reporting**: Daily, Monthly

### Bed Utilization

#### Bed Occupancy Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of available beds occupied
- **Calculation**: Occupied Bed Days ÷ Available Bed Days × 100
- **Target**: 85-92%
- **Reporting**: Daily

#### Occupied Bed Days (OBD)
- **Definition**: Total number of days beds were occupied
- **Calculation**: Sum of inpatient days across all beds
- **Reporting**: Daily, Monthly

#### Available Bed Days (ABD)
- **Definition**: Total potential bed days if all beds occupied
- **Calculation**: Number of beds × Days in period
- **Reporting**: Monthly

### Operating Room Metrics

#### OR Utilization
- **Definition**: Percentage of scheduled OR time used
- **Calculation**: Actual procedure time ÷ Scheduled OR time × 100
- **Target**: 80-85%
- **Reporting**: Weekly

#### First Case On-Time Start
- **Definition**: Percentage of first cases starting within 5 minutes of scheduled time
- **Target**: ≥ 85%
- **Reporting**: Daily

#### Turnover Time
- **Definition**: Time between patient out and next patient in
- **Target**: < 30 minutes
- **Reporting**: Daily

## Patient Experience Metrics

### HCAHPS Scores

#### Overall Hospital Rating
- **Definition**: Patient rating of hospital 0-10
- **Target**: Top decile performance (≥ 9)
- **Reporting**: Monthly

#### Key HCAHPS Dimensions
| Dimension | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Nurse Communication | ≥ 85% always |
| Doctor Communication | ≥ 85% always |
| Responsiveness | ≥ 75% always |
| Pain Management | ≥ 75% always |
| Medication Communication | ≥ 70% always |
| Discharge Information | ≥ 90% yes |
| Cleanliness | ≥ 80% always |
| Quietness | ≥ 65% always |

### Patient Complaints
- **Definition**: Formal complaints received
- **Calculation**: Complaints per 1,000 patient days
- **Target**: < 2.0 per 1,000 patient days
- **Resolution Target**: 100% within 7 business days
- **Reporting**: Monthly

## Financial Metrics

### Case Mix Index (CMI)
- **Definition**: Average relative weight of DRGs
- **Interpretation**: Higher CMI indicates more complex cases
- **Reporting**: Monthly

### Cost per Case
- **Definition**: Total operating costs ÷ Number of cases
- **Target**: Below regional benchmark
- **Reporting**: Monthly, Quarterly

### Revenue per Patient Day
- **Definition**: Total revenue ÷ Patient days
- **Reporting**: Monthly
